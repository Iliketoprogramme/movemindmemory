Our Vision

With Move Mind Memory,

Manifest Meaningful Moments and Maintain Dignity.

Loss of Life

Alzheimer patients lose not only their memories, but their lives; they forget how to chew food, get into their bed and even how to breathe.

Is Becoming Our Problem

Mortality rates related to Alzheimer's have increased by 140% during the past 20 years. That indeed is a sharp increase.

So, We...

have developed Move Mind Memory - the app which aims to address cognitive enhancement and tackle hygiene issues for the patients to live alone and with dignity.

Features

Every Move Matters.

Prevent patients' cognitive abilities from deteriorating at a much faster rate.

AI-Based Motion Tracking

Stimulates both brain and body to promote cognitive and motor function retention

Activities for Daily Essential Movements

Hygiene: brushing teeth, washing hands, showering, bathing.

Eating and medication activities: training fine motor skills (ex. Using chopsticks)

Voice Support

Verbal instructions combining auditory and visual prompts

Gamification

Positive reinforcement.

Reward system with tokens.
